FAQ

Questions clients usually ask

Everything you need to know before we work together.

What makes you different from other VAs?
I don't just complete tasks — I take ownership of outcomes. With 8+ years of experience across finance, real estate, and contracting, I understand how businesses actually operate. I build systems, not just to-do lists, and I proactively identify problems before they reach you.
What time zone do you work in?
I'm based in Cebu City, Philippines (PHT, UTC+8), but I'm highly flexible and have experience working across US, Australian, and UK time zones. We can align overlap hours during our discovery call to make sure communication is seamless.
How do we get started?
Simple — book a free 15-minute discovery call. We'll talk about where you're stuck, what you need, and which package fits best. From there I'll send a simple agreement and we can typically start within 2–3 business days.
Do you require a long-term contract?
No lock-in contracts. I work on a flexible hourly basis so you can scale up, scale down, or pause as your business needs change. I earn your continued business every week — not through paperwork.
How do you handle confidential information?
Confidentiality is non-negotiable for me. I'm comfortable signing NDAs and have handled sensitive financial, client, and operational data for years. Data security and discretion are built into how I work — not an afterthought.
What if I'm not happy with the work?
I take feedback seriously and will always revise or redo work until it meets your standards. I do weekly check-ins so nothing drifts for long. If things aren't working out, you can end the arrangement with no penalties.
Can you work with my existing team?
Absolutely. I'm experienced in collaborating with internal teams, other VAs, and external vendors. I adapt to your existing communication style and tools — I'm here to support, not disrupt.
What tools do you use to track hours and tasks?
I use whichever project management tool your team prefers — Asana, Trello, Airtable, or even a shared Google Sheet. For time tracking I use tools like Toggl or Clockify and send regular summaries so you always know exactly where hours are going.
How quickly do you respond to messages?
During agreed working hours, you can expect responses within 1–2 hours for standard requests and same-day turnaround for most tasks. For Executive tier clients, I prioritize same-day responses even for complex deliverables.
Can I upgrade or change my plan later?
Yes — anytime. Many clients start with the Starter plan to test the waters and upgrade once they see the impact. There's no penalty to change tiers; we just adjust the hours and scope going forward.
